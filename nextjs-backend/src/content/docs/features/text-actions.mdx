Tabby provides a suite of specialized tools for transforming selected text. These are accessible via the Text Agent in the Homescreen grid or by searching for them directly in the Action Menu.

## Core Text Tools

### Fix Grammar
Analyzes your text for spelling, punctuation, and grammatical errors. It corrects them while maintaining your original tone and style. Perfect for a final check of emails or documentation.

### Shorten and Expand
- **Shorten**: Condenses your text by removing filler words and redundancy while preserving all key information.
- **Expand**: Adds relevant details, examples, and context to flesh out your points.

## Tone Adjustments

Transform the tone of your writing instantly without changing the underlying message:

- **Professional**: Rewrites text for business environments, suitable for formal emails and reports.
- **Casual**: Makes the tone more relaxed and conversational, ideal for team chats or personal notes.
- **Friendly**: Adds warmth and approachability to your message.

## Specialized Tools

### Email Writer
Converts bullet points or rough thoughts into a polished, well-structured email with appropriate greetings and sign-offs.

### Custom Prompt
Gives you full control. You can provide any instruction like "Rewrite this in the style of a technical manual" or "Convert this list into a JSON object".

---

## How to Use

1. Select text in any application.
2. Open the Action Menu (**Ctrl + \\**).
3. Select **Text Agent** to see the tool grid, or type the name of the tool (e.g., "Grammar") to trigger it directly.
